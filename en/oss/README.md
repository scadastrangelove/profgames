## What This Is

`en/oss/` is the English version of the OSS/JITI package: a manifesto-style article plus a machine-readable layer of **110 signals** across OSS, music, publishing, data commons, law, security disclosure and cloud/runtime.

Core frame: the old software equilibrium rested on three things: copying was expensive, origin was provable, and violators were reachable. AI simultaneously makes copying cheap, blurs artifact provenance, and shifts enforcement from law toward platforms. Rent therefore moves not to whoever creates the artifact, but to whoever controls entry, execution and trust.
